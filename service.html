Our Services

End-to-End Workforce Sourcing & Deployment Support for Europe

We provide complete sourcing, interview coordination, training, and deployment support to European companies. Our services are designed to ensure a smooth, compliant, and efficient hiring process from worker selection to successful arrival and placement in Europe.

European Company Interview Coordination

We arrange and manage interview programs for European companies traveling to India for worker selection.

  • Arrangement of interview boards to travel to India
  • Assistance with visa processing for European company representatives
  • Coordination of travel schedules and local transportation
  • Arrangement of hotel accommodation and logistics
  • On-ground support to ensure smooth interview operations

Interview Center Arrangement

We organize professional interview centers to conduct worker interviews and trade tests efficiently.

  • Fully equipped interview centers in India
  • Coordination of worker attendance and interview schedules
  • Arrangement of technical trade tests and skill demonstrations
  • Documentation support and candidate shortlisting
  • End-to-end interview management to save employer time

Pre-Departure Training (European Standards)

Before deployment, workers undergo training aligned with European workplace standards.

  • Job-specific technical skill training
  • European safety rules and compliance orientation
  • Introduction to European work culture and expectations
  • Basic communication and workplace behavior guidance
  • Final readiness assessment before travel

Worker Visa Assistance & Documentation Support

We assist workers throughout the visa application process to ensure compliance and timely deployment.

  • Guidance on visa requirements and documentation
  • Support with application preparation and submission
  • Coordination with authorized recruitment partners
  • Pre-departure briefings for workers
  • Ensuring legal and procedural compliance

Ongoing Support & Coordination

  • Continuous coordination between European companies and workers
  • Support for future workforce requirements
  • Long-term workforce relationship management
  • Assistance with replacement or expansion needs

Our Commitment

We are committed to providing reliable, transparent, and professional services, helping European companies hire skilled workers efficiently while ensuring workers are fully prepared for successful employment in Europe.
