ABOUT US

Scandic Roots is a Slovenia-registered business consulting and skilled workforce sourcing company dedicated to supporting European companies in the shipbuilding, construction, and transport & logistics sectors. We specialize in sourcing qualified, experienced, and job-ready workers from Asian countries including expert shipbuilding and marine specialists, skilled construction tradespeople, and professional truck and trailer drivers to meet your temporary, long-term, and project-based manpower requirements.

Through our strong international sourcing network and rigorous vetting process, we deliver reliable, compliant workforce solutions that fully adhere to European quality, safety, labour, and regulatory standards. Our goal is to help clients maintain operational efficiency, meet demanding project timelines, and stay competitive in challenging industries.

  • Slovenia-registered & EU-compliant operations
  • Specialized sourcing for shipbuilding, construction & logistics
  • Access to experienced Asian workforce pools
  • Temporary, long-term & project-based staffing solutions
  • Full compliance with European labour & safety standards
  • Fast, reliable & transparent recruitment process
  • Personalized support & ongoing coordination
